CleanCopy. Paste anywhere.

A lightweight Mac utility that instantly strips formatting off any text you copy, with a familiar command.

How it works

  1. Highlight your text
  2. Hit ⌘C + C
  3. Paste anywhere

Why

  • Stop fighting formatting when pasting into docs, email, or chat.
  • Works everywhere on Mac, system-wide.
  • Lightning-fast: runs silently in the background.
  • One shortcut you’ll never forget.
